Cash and Carries are a cornerstone of Peckham, keeping local businesses and families thriving in the area. local independent owners pushing back against the private equity giants trying to take over the highstreet. preserving the spirit of the people and retaining the cultural identities of the many sub communities here. this photo series aims to capture that spirit, displaying the warmth and familiarity of these shops.
